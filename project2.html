Project 2: Companies & Products ideas/briefs

Knowhere Universal Market

The company market is a universal brand that is known in the whole universe. The products sold from here will sell the most iconic items from the Marvel Cinematic Universe, ranging from equipment to cosmic artifacts that were used by heroes and villians. Popular items include like the vibranium shield, arc reactor, Thor's hammer, Infinity Gauntlet, and the Tesseract.

DriveHaven

This company sells a variety of car accessory products that will enchnace your experince whenever you are on the go while making convenient for yourself and passengers. The products sold on here will be stuff like trunk organizers, seat gap fillers, car chargers, car vacuums, and mounts for holding your devices while focusing on the road.

Atlas Carry

A company with one focus in mind, to travel easier while making it very accessible and less costly as possible. This primary focuses on air travel as to help customers to travel with as much as they can while trying to limit any excess amount to carry, thus avoiding baggage fees. The products that will mostly be shown on here would will mostly travel backpacks, luggages, duffle bags, packing cubes, organizers, and compression bags.
